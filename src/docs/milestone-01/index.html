Overview

Prix is a very helpful web application that allows online shoppers to do all their browsing all in one site. Gone are the days of looking up the price of certain products one site at a time and manually comparing which one is cheapest, or has the best deals. With Prix, shoppers will be able to not only search for any product on the internet, but will also be given price comparisons, additional information on the product including shipping and/or possible deals, and the ability to filter out and sort products with given criteria. When the shopper finds a product they like, a link will be availableto direct them to the external site for purchase. Find whatever your heart desires, all in one tab, with Prix.

Application Parts

Filtering and Sorting Options:

Users of Prix will have access to filtering and sorting in order to enhance their shopping experience. Results can be sorted by date of release (earliest to latest, latest to earliest), popularity, price (highest to lowest, lowest to highest), etc. Results can also be filtered based on a variety of factors, such as price ranges, modes and rates of shipping, location, customer ratings, etc

Product Detail Frames:

When Prix users search up a product, the results will be shown in an informative and clean manner. Each individual product will have its own frame which will display important information, such as price, original product site, the number of confirmed purchases, and more. Additional information will be available and displayed when the user hovers over the frame.

Product Comparison Tool:

When users find a certain product they may consider purchasing, they can click the product to save it to the Product Comparison Tool. If more than one product is selected, our tool will automatically create a comprehensive report that will list the individual products’ information, highlight pros and cons, and give our recommendation on which product to purchase, via our own algorithm. Data Requirements

Product info

The foundation of our application lies in comprehensive product information sourced from multiple clothing shopping websites. This includes details such as product name, description, brand, size availability, color options, material composition, and pricing. Ensuring the accuracy and completeness of this data is crucial for enabling users to make informed purchasing decisions. Our wireframe for product information displays a structured layout, emphasizing clarity and ease of navigation, ensuring users can quickly access vital details about each item.

User preference

Understanding user preferences is essential for delivering personalized recommendations. We collect data on user preferences, including preferred price range, material preferences (cotton, polyester), preferred colors, preferred brands, and any specific keywords or styles users are searching for. By leveraging this information, we can tailor search results to match each user's unique preferences. The wireframe for user preferences features intuitive controls and customizable options, empowering users to refine their search criteria effortlessly.

Data

Our application relies on scraping data from various clothing shopping websites to gather real-time pricing information and product availability. This data includes product listings, prices, discounts, and stock availability. We continuously update and monitor this data to ensure that users are presented with the most current and accurate information available. The wireframe for data collection showcases an efficient data retrieval process, emphasizing speed and reliability to provide users with up to date information seamlessly.

Search Query

Analyzing user search queries provides valuable insights into emerging trends and popular clothing items. We collect and analyze search query data to identify patterns, popular brands, trending styles, and frequently searched keywords. This information helps us optimize our search algorithms and enhance the overall user experience. The wireframe for search query analysis displays a user-friendly interface for exploring search trends, enabling users to discover new styles and stay informed about the latest fashion trends effortlessly.

Real-World Connection

In our world today, life is so fast-paced and dynamic, with our days full of ToDo’s and responsibilities. With the addition of the internet, we humans simply don’t have the time to sift through countless sites to find the best deals on products and services. This is inefficient, daunting, and sometimes straight up annoying. Prix offers a solution by taking the work out of online shopping, whilst maintaining the thrill and excitement of finally finding THE one. Anyone can experience the services of Prix, from fresh undergraduates to experienced employees; from first-time parents to teachers and caretakers. Our simple, informative, and accessible website can assist anyone in finding the right product for their needs, minus the time wasted scrolling through different websites and tabs.
