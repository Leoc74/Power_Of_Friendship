The Price Comparison Engine

What is Prix?

Prix is a very helpful web application that allows online shoppers to do all their browsing all in one site. Gone are the days of looking up the price of certain products one site at a time and manually comparing which one is cheapest, or has the best deals. With Prix, shoppers will be able to not only search for any product on the internet, but will also be given price comparisons, additional information on the product including shipping and/or possible deals, and the ability to filter out and sort products with given criteria. When the shopper finds a product they like, a link will be available to direct them to the external site for purchase. Find whatever your heart desires, all in one tab, with Prix.
